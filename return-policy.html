RETURN POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to BenardFarms Fashion. We value your satisfaction. This Return Policy explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds are handled for products purchased from our store.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7–14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-made outfits
  • Altered or tailored clothing
  • Sale or discounted items (if stated)
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

3. Return Process

  • Contact our support team before returning any item
  • Provide order details and reason for return
  • Wait for approval before shipping items back
  • Approved returns must be sent to our return address

4. Refund Policy

Once your returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 3–7 business days
  • Refunds will be sent via the original payment method

5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect.

6. Shipping Costs

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs (unless item is faulty)
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable

7. Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately for resolution.
